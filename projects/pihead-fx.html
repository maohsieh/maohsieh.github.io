pihead-fx~

pihead-fx~ is a multi-effect guitar signal processor built on a Raspberry Pi, leveraging Pure Data (Pd) DSP for real-time audio processing. This project integrates DSP programming, UI/UX design, hardware prototyping, and 3D modeling into a cohesive, fully functional amp/effects modeler.

Developed as my senior project at UC San Diego under the guidance of Professor Tom Erbe, pihead-fx~ was conceptualized and designed in early 2019 and completed in June of the same year. The goal was to create a modular, software-driven effects unit that retains the hands-on interaction of traditional guitar pedals while offering the flexibility of digital signal processing.
