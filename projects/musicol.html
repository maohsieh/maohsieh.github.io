MusiCol

MusiCol is a startup project for a mobile app dedicated to connecting musicians and enhancing their experience in collaborating online.

Developed during UCSD’s "Successful Entrepreneurship for Engineers" course, I co-managed a team of five, set project goals, organized team meetings, and co-authored a business proposal.

The idea emerged from the challenges musicians faced during the COVID-19 pandemic—isolated, unable to perform or record together. I proposed MusiCol: a social platform enabling musicians to collaborate asynchronously from their homes, combining my passions for music and design.

As the lead UI/UX designer, I created a fully interactive prototype using Figma, focusing on musical profiles, asynchronous demo recording, and a matchmaking system.

This project introduced me to entrepreneurship from ideation through pitch delivery, helping me build skills in design, business strategy, and remote teamwork.
